MAKING MORE BIKE RIDERS IN KENTUCKIANA

We strive to get more people riding bicycles in Kentuckiana, using a three-pronged approach:

  • Advocate

    We work with government, businesses, and other organizations to plan and construct better bicycle infrastructure including trails, greenways, and bike lanes.

  • Educate

    We provide education to the motorists and bike riders in our community on safe and legal riding techniques that will make it possible to ride on existing city streets or to share the road with those who do.

  • Ride

    We provide reasons for people to get out and ride.
